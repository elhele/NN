Q: Formulate the principle of topographic map formation.

A: The spatial location of an output neuron in a topographic map corresponds to a particular domain of data drawn from the input space.

Q: Name the essential processes involved in the formation of SOM.

A: Competition, cooperation, synaptic adaptation.

Q: How large can be a neuron's neighborhood in a convergence phase of the adaptive process?

A: It can only contain the nearest neighbors of the neuron or even be of a zero radius - contain only one neuron.
