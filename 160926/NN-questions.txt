Q: Name the main capabilities of a neural network.

A: Adaptivity, input-output mapping, evidental response, fault tolerance, contextual knowledge representation, implementation using very large scale integration, uniformity of analysis and design.


Q: What are synapses?

A: Synapses are basic structural and functional units of interaction between the neurons.


Q: Name 3 network architectures.

A: Single-layer feedforward, multilayer feedforward, reccurent.
